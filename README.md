## Customer Satisfaction & Feedback Analysis
#Market Research Project
# Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer satisfaction and feedback survey data to understand the key factors that influence customer satisfaction and loyalty.
The main objective is to convert raw survey data into meaningful insights that can support business and client decision-making.

This project is designed in a market research and client analytics context, similar to real-world survey analysis work.

## Project Objectives

Understand overall customer satisfaction levels

Identify key drivers of customer satisfaction

Analyze the impact of service quality, product quality, and loyalty on satisfaction

Generate actionable business recommendations based on survey insights

# Dataset Description

Dataset Type: Customer Feedback / Satisfaction Survey

Total Records: ~38,000

Total Columns: 11

Key Columns Used:

Customer Age

Gender

Country

Satisfaction Score

Service Quality

Product Quality

Loyalty Level

Income

## Tools & Technologies Used

Python – Main language for data analysis

Pandas – Data cleaning, manipulation, and analysis

Matplotlib – Data visualization (charts & graphs)

Excel – Data review, validation, and reporting

SQL – Data filtering and aggregation (for validation)

## Methodology

Data understanding and column analysis

Data cleaning (handling missing and inconsistent values)

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Customer segmentation based on:

Age group

Gender

Country

Loyalty level

Visualization of satisfaction trends

Insight generation and business interpretation

## Key Analysis Performed

Country-wise customer satisfaction analysis

Age-wise and gender-wise satisfaction trends

Service quality vs customer satisfaction

Loyalty level impact on satisfaction

Identification of high and low satisfaction customer segments

 #Key Insights

Service quality has is one of the strongest drivers of customer satisfaction

Customers with higher loyalty levels show higher satisfaction scores

Income level has relatively less impact compared to service experience

Medium-loyal customers are a key segment for improvement

## Business Recommendations

Improve service quality to increase overall customer satisfaction

Focus on loyalty programs to convert medium-loyal customers into high-loyal customers

Use survey insights regularly to monitor customer experience

## Visualizations
